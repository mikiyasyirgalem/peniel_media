Who is peniel Media and Studio?

peniel Media and Studio is a media where different services of the Ethiopian Orthodox Church are prepared and presented.

When and how was peniel Media and Studio established?

Peniel Media and Studio was founded and started its work in 2016 e.c .

Peniel Media, although it started working in 2016e.c with full capacity, but for a long time it was thought and tested by the owner/founder. But for a variety of reasons, Saiska has been around for a long time.

However, after many trials and tribulations, he was able to succeed and start his work in 2016e.c .
